Experimental route optimisation system for multi-stop delivery work. Explores practical routing strategies and real-world constraints rather than purely theoretical optimisation.

Early-stage and primarily a research and learning project.

---

## Overview

- **Platform:** iOS (SwiftUI, minimum iOS 17)
- **Approach:** Adaptive Large Neighbourhood Search (ALNS) for route optimisation
- **Focus:** Real-world delivery constraints — time windows, vehicle capacity, road suitability
- **Status:** Private, early-stage development
